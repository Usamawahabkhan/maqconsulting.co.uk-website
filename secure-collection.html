SECURE IT DISPOSAL COLLECTION

MAQ Consulting Ltd have their own fleet of secure vehicles to undertake customer collections. We also have a dedicated logistics partner, enabling us to collect IT from anywhere in the UK at the best possible rates; without compromising the risks associated with transporting redundant IT Equipment. Our dedicated collection services, ensures the transport for our customer’s equipment is handled to the highest levels.


Every collection undertaken, uses secure plastic pallet boxes to ensure items, once scanned and located in boxes, cannot be tampered with on their journey to our facility. The customer can be reassured the items are not only shipped on a dedicated collection vehicle but also sealed in secure pallets. This guarantees our secure collection service is the most reliable and resilient in the ITAD industry. If you have smaller collections and decide to use our secure logistics partner, we will receive the goods and then decant the IT kit into plastic tubs on arrival at our facility. This allows for safe and secure racking until they go for initial assessment.


Recycling old computers is no longer about scrapping the devices or just diverting the waste from landfill. IT recycling is about maximising revenue returns, controlling and managing risk and ensuring all security aspects of the service are safe and approved. We ensure our customers receive a quality service, where they feel their data is secure and that they have a trusted partner who will guide them through the ever changing legal and environmental compliance issues associated with IT disposal.



To provide a quote we simply need to know:

- How many items you want Recycling, ideally a list.

- Location of the equipment?

- If you want the devices remarketed or securely Disposed ?

- What is the collection post code?
